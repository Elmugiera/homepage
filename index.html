About Me

I am deeply passionate about supporting vulnerable individuals and strengthening my local community. My work with asylum seekers involves providing crucial assistance and guidance as they navigate the complexities of the asylum process. I find it incredibly rewarding to help them feel safe and empowered during a challenging time in their lives. Additionally, I dedicate time to training new volunteers, equipping them with the skills and understanding necessary to make a meaningful impact. Witnessing their growth and commitment is truly inspiring. Beyond these roles, I actively seek out opportunities to support my community in various ways, whether through local initiatives or lending a hand where it's needed most. I believe in the power of connection and collective action to create a more compassionate and supportive environment for everyone.

What I Do

  • Asylum Guides Coordinator
  • Arabic interpreter - various agencies
  • Volunteer Teacher - Wolverhampton
